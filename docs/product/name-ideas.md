# Name Ideas

## Direction 1: direct and clear

### 1. Build Your Life in Himachal
Strong, emotional, broad enough to become a platform.

### 2. Life in Himachal
Simple and clear, but a bit generic.

### 3. Himachal Base
Short and product-like. Good if we want a more modern startup feel.

### 4. Himachal Match
Very productized, works well for the quiz phase, but narrower emotionally.

## Direction 2: warmer / more editorial

### 5. The Himachal Life
Feels a little more media/editorial.

### 6. Mountain Base
Broad and elegant, but less clearly Himachal.

### 7. Home in Himachal
Warm, intimate, emotionally strong.

### 8. Settle in Himachal
Very clear, practical, slightly more relocation-specific.

## Direction 3: local / poetic

### 9. Pahad Base
Memorable and more rooted, but narrower depending on audience.

### 10. Pahad Wala Life
Has personality, but may be too informal for a broad product.

### 11. Cedar & Sky
Beautiful vibe, but too abstract for initial clarity.

### 12. Ghar in Himachal
Warm and rooted, especially emotionally resonant for return-to-Himachal users.

## My top recommendations

### Best brand-level name
**Build Your Life in Himachal**

Why:
- emotionally resonant
- broad enough for long-term platform expansion
- aligned with the product vision
- distinctive

### Best product-like alternative
**Himachal Base**

Why:
- cleaner and shorter
- easier as an app/product label
- good if we want the brand to feel more like a tool

### Warmest alternative
**Home in Himachal**

Why:
- emotionally sticky
- stronger if the long-term focus becomes relocation and belonging

## Recommendation
Use:
- **Brand / umbrella:** Build Your Life in Himachal
- **Product shorthand internally:** Himachal Base
