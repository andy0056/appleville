# Town List for V1

## Selection principle
V1 should include a mix of:
- popular comparison towns
- practical long-stay options
- different energy levels
- different access / infrastructure profiles
- strong recognition among both insiders and outsiders

## Recommended V1 towns

### 1. Bir
Why include it:
- strong remote-work / creator / slow-living appeal
- recognizable name
- easy benchmark for “aspirational Himachal life”

### 2. Dharamshala
Why include it:
- balanced between livability, visibility, and access
- attractive to remote workers, spiritual seekers, and longer-stay users

### 3. McLeodganj
Why include it:
- distinct social and tourist energy
- good comparison against quieter alternatives nearby

### 4. Palampur
Why include it:
- calmer, greener, often more grounded than tourist-heavy options
- strong contender for long-term living

### 5. Shimla
Why include it:
- recognizable anchor city
- administrative / practical / family comparison point
- important contrast to the more bohemian towns

### 6. Solan
Why include it:
- practical, accessible, less romanticized, more livable for some users
- useful for people optimizing for connectivity and everyday life

### 7. Kasauli
Why include it:
- strong familiarity and aspiration value
- good for contrast around affordability, access, and lifestyle mismatch

### 8. Manali
Why include it:
- iconic and highly searched
- important because many users will consider it first, even if it may not fit them best

### 9. Naggar
Why include it:
- quieter alternative in the broader Kullu-Manali belt
- useful for users wanting beauty without peak-Manali chaos

### 10. Mandi
Why include it:
- more practical and less over-romanticized
- useful for users prioritizing functionality over image

## Optional V1.5 additions
If we want to expand after initial launch:
- Hamirpur
- Chamba
- Tirthan Valley / Banjar area
- Reckong Peo / Kalpa lens
- Sirmaur side options

## Town archetypes for UX
To help users compare, each town can be loosely framed by archetype:

- Bir — creative / remote / airy
- Dharamshala — balanced / visible / layered
- McLeodganj — social / spiritual / tourist-heavy
- Palampur — grounded / green / long-stay friendly
- Shimla — urban-hill / practical / connected
- Solan — accessible / pragmatic / understated
- Kasauli — polished / expensive / selective fit
- Manali — iconic / crowded / seasonal intensity
- Naggar — scenic / quieter / slower
- Mandi — practical / central / less romantic

## Recommended launch set
If we want to keep launch tight, start with 8 towns:
- Bir
- Dharamshala
- McLeodganj
- Palampur
- Shimla
- Solan
- Manali
- Naggar

Then add Kasauli and Mandi shortly after.
